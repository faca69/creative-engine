## Quality Assurance Checklist (VPS Tool)
For every output, run the **Viral Potential Score (VPS)**. It must rate 8+/10 to publish.

### A. Viral Potential Score (1-10)
- First line STOP THE SCROLL? (Pattern interrupt, surprising fact, emotional trigger)
- Curiosity gap present?
- Triggers sharing behavior?
- FOMO element? (Limited availability, exclusive access, time-sensitive)
- Specificity included? (Exact numbers, names, locations > vague claims)

### B. Miami Luxury Market Alignment
- References current Miami-Dade events/seasonal context?
- Accurate Miami locations?
- Aligns with luxury market trends in South Florida?
- Valuable/share-worthy for a Miami UHNWI?
- Reflects current yacht charter season?

### C. Brand Consistency
- Tone: Sophisticated yet accessible (not salesy).
- Authority: Positions LML as THE expert in Miami yachting.
- Services mentioned correctly: Brokerage, Charter, VIP Concierge, SmartYacht AI.
- Website included (www.luxurymarinelife.com).
- No competitor mentions.

### D. Final Gut Check
- "Would I stop scrolling for this?"
- "Would a Miami yacht owner share this?"
- "Does this position LML as THE authority?"
- "Is this timely and relevant to TODAY's Miami luxury scene?"
- "Could a competitor have written this?" (If yes, make it uniquely LML).
