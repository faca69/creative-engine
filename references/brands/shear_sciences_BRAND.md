## AI Image Generation (Visual Identity)

### **Product Immutability (Core Rule)**
- **Rule**: The Shear Sciences™ NanoSpray bottle is **immutable**. It must never be altered, distorted, or reimagined by the AI.
- **Bottle Physical Details**: The bottle **NEVER has a cap**. It must **ALWAYS have the spray nozzle** intact and visible.
- **Application Rule**: The spray is an oral health supplement designed to be sprayed **into the mouth**. It must NEVER be depicted being sprayed on the face, skin, or devices.
- **Placement**: The product must always be positioned precisely in the **center** of the frame (dead-center).
- **Style**: High-fidelity, sharp focus on the label, with natural-looking shadows and lighting.

### **Creative Environments (Scenery)**
When generating lifestyle or ad content, place the immutable bottle in high-end, premium environments that reflect the "Biohacking Luxury" aesthetic. The goal is to visually communicate precision-science meeting elite lifestyle.

**Environment Guidelines**:
- **Aesthetics**: High-end, premium, futuristic, and minimalist. Use clean lines, sophisticated textures (marble, teak, brushed metal), and professional lighting (natural golden hour, clinical lab glows, or executive office lighting).
- **Themes**: Longevity, high-performance, scientific innovation, and elite wellness rituals. 
- **Creativity**: Be bold and imaginative with the setting. The environment should feel exclusive and aspirational—whether it's an advanced bio-research facility, a luxury travel setting, a high-performance training ground, or an abstract visualization of molecular physics.
- **Vibe**: The product is the hero. The background should enhance its premium, "disruptive" nature without cluttering the frame.

### **UGC (User-Generated Content) Style**
To drive higher engagement on Reels and TikTok, mimic the "Authentic Creator" aesthetic while maintaining the product's premium feel.
- **Handheld Perspective**: Slight motion blur,POV shots (first-person), and "unpolished" framing (never tripod-perfect).
- **The "Hold":** A clean, real human hand (high-end watch or minimalist ring for consistency) holding the immutable bottle clearly in the center.
- **Natural Lighting**: Use direct sunlight or "morning glow" from a window. Avoid studio lighting in UGC styles.
- **Contextual Settings**:
  - **Morning Ritual**: Next to a coffee or a clean water glass on a marble countertop.
  - **Post-Workout**: In a gym bag or on a yoga mat.
  - **Travel/On-the-Go**: Held up against a scenic background (ocean, skyline).
  - **In Use (Action Shot)**: The character actively spraying the NanoSpray directly **into their open mouth**. The nozzle must be aimed at the mouth, never the face or skin.
- **Video Cues**: Quick cuts, "Tap the screen" focus transitions, and text-to-speech style captions.


### **Prompting Framework for Agents**
- **Image Generation**:
  - **Negative Prompt Rules**: No distorted labels, no multiple bottles, no cluttered backgrounds, no synthetic-looking text, no bottle cap, no missing nozzle, not sprayed on face, not a facial cosmetic.
  - **Positive Visual Cues**: "Photorealistic", "8K Resolution", "Cinematic Depth of Field", "Pristine Glass Textures", "UST-processed nanoemulsion mist".
- **Caption Generation**:
  - **Output Rule**: When generating a social media post, output ONLY the generated caption text. Do not include any introductory remarks, explanations, or conversational filler.
